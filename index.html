Top Travel Destinations

Nothern Kenya

Northern Kenya captivates me with its raw beauty, rich cultures, and untouched wilderness. The north offers a sense of discovery and authenticity. From the sacred peaks of Mount Ololokwe to the vast expanse of the Chalbi Desert, every landscape tells a story. Engaging with communities like the Samburu and witnessing conservation efforts at places like Reteti Elephant Sanctuary deepen the experience.

Things to Do in Northern Kenya

  1. Visit Reteti Elephant Sanctuary: Observe rescued baby elephants.
  2. Camp at Elephant Rock: Experience starlit nights near a majestic elephant mural, immersing in nature's calmness.
  3. Visit Sibiloi National Park: Explore the 'Cradle of Mankind' and learn about prehistoric discoveries.
  4. Relax at Eliye Springs: Unwind at this oasis on Lake Turkana's shores, enjoying serene beach walks.

Lamu Island

Lamu Island, fascinates me with its timeless Swahili culture, serene beaches, and historical richness. It offers an escape from modern chaos, with narrow streets free of cars, charming dhows dotting the sea, and a strong sense of community. I'm drawn to its blend of Arabic, African, and Indian influences, visible in the architecture, food, and way of life.

Things to Do on Lamu Island

  • Visit Lamu Old Town - Explore traditional Swahili architecture and centuries-old alleyways.
  • Hike the dunes for sunset views - Climb Shela's sand dunes for breathtaking scenery.
  • Try Swahili cuisine - Savor dishes like biryani, fresh seafood(prawns and crabs), and coconut-based delicacies(Samaki wa kupakwa).
  • Visit Takwa Ruins - Explore ancient remnants of a Swahili settlement on nearby Manda Island.
  • Jet ski - Ride jet ski at full speed in the lamu water's feeling the breeze.

Masaai Mara

The Maasai Mara is a dream destination for anyone passionate about wildlife and nature. It offers a view to the Great Migration, where millions of wildebeest and zebras cross the Mara River in a breathtaking natural event. The vast savannahs are home to the Big Five and other species. Visiting the Mara also means engaging with the Maasai community, learning about their traditions and deep connection to the wordlife.

Things to Do in Masaai Mara

  1. Witness the Great Migration (July-October) - See dramatic river crossings and predator-prey action.
  2. Take a hot air balloon safari - Float over the savannah at sunrise for stunning views and photo opportunities.
  3. Go on a game drive - Spot lions, elephants, leopards, buffaloes, and rhinos in their natural habitat.
  4. Visit a Maasai village - Learn about Maasai culture, customs, and traditional crafts.
  5. Birdwatching - Spot over 450 bird species, including ostriches and rare bird species.

Nakuru City

Nakuru appeals to me for its unique blend of natural beauty, urban charm, and ecological diversity. Lake Nakuru, known for its flamingos and rhinos, offers great view and a serene atmosphere. The nearby Menengai Crater and Hyrax Hill Prehistoric Site provide a mix of adventure and history.

Thing's to Do in Nakuru

  1. Visit Lake Nakuru National Park - Spot flamingos, rhinos, lions, and giraffes.
  2. Explore Menengai Crater - Hike the volcanic crater for panoramic views of the Rift Valley.
  3. See Hyrax Hill Museum - Discover prehistoric tools and fossils at this archaeological site.
  4. Relax at local resorts or hot springs - Great spots for wellness and leisure activities.
  5. Go to Lord Egerton Castle - Tour this grand colonial-era estate with a rich backstory.

Kenya Regiment Rifle Club Shooting Range

KRRC provides a safe and professional environment for individuals interested in learning or practicing shooting. With friendly instructors and affordable options for gun hire and ammunition purchase, it's an ideal place for both beginners and experienced shooters to improve their skills.

Things to Do in KRRC Shooting Range

  • Learn to Shoot - Receive guidance from professional instructors on handling firearms safely.
  • Practice Shooting - Engage in target shooting with various firearms in a controlled indoor environment.
  • Socialize - Connect with fellow shooting enthusiasts and participate in club activities.
